Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a listing?

Simply use the "Post Your Own Listing" form above. Select whether you are buying or selling, enter the crop details, your price expectations, and location. Once submitted, your listing will appear on the live board instantly.

Is there a commission on trades?

No, AgroTrack acts strictly as a marketplace connector. There are zero commissions on trades. You coordinate delivery and payment directly with the buyer/seller.

How do I contact a buyer/seller?

Click the "Contact Buyer" or "Contact Seller" button on their listing. If you are a free user, you will get their phone number or email to contact. Pro users get integrated in-app messaging and negotiation tools.
