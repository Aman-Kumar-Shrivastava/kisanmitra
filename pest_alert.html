Prevention Tips

Timely Spraying

Apply recommended pesticides early morning or evening for maximum effectiveness and minimal crop stress.

Crop Rotation

Rotate crops every season to break pest breeding cycles and reduce soil-borne infestations naturally.

Regular Scouting

Walk your fields weekly and check traps. Early detection saves upto 80% of potential crop loss.

Water Management

Avoid waterlogging as it attracts pests. Use drip irrigation to maintain optimal soil moisture levels.
